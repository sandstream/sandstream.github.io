ABOUT THIS PROJECT

Transparency about what this is and how it was made.

WHAT IS THIS?

Diesel Johnny Raines is not a real person.

He is an art project — a fictional artist created through collaboration between human creativity and AI tools. I wanted to explore whether it's possible to create genuine emotional resonance through a fictional story.

The music is real. The emotions are real. The person is not.

WHY?

I've always been fascinated by storytelling. How music can carry a narrative. How a character can feel real even when we know they're fictional.

With AI tools, I wanted to see if I could create an entire world — an artist with a history, a family, loss, and rebuilding. Three albums that tell one continuous story.

Diesel isn't real. But the feelings in his music are.

HOW WAS THIS CREATED?

  • 📝
    Story & Lyrics
    Developed with Claude Code and the BMAD Method. Characters, family history, album arc — built like a screenplay for a fictional trilogy.
  • 🎵
    Music
    Created with Suno AI. Lyrics written based on the character's emotional journey. Each album has its own sonic identity.
  • 📷
    Images
    Generated with Sora. Designed to feel like real family photos and candid shots, not polished press photos.
  • 🎨
    Production & Distribution
    Audio processed with ffmpeg. Distributed via DistroKid. Website built with Jekyll and hosted on Vercel.

MY REFLECTION

This project taught me a lot about storytelling, about AI as a creative tool, and about the line between "real" and "created."

Is Diesel's grief real? No, he doesn't exist.

Can you still feel it? I hope so.

Art has always been about creating emotions through fiction. The question isn't whether Diesel is real — it's whether his story moves you.
